Notes for why the extension might be misbehaving or not working

        - The extension is inactive on installation. Check the power button at the top right corner; its color is blue when active.

        - The extension needs to download metagame data from GitHub at least once; it can't work otherwise.

        - Only the National Dex Anything Goes format, and above (e.g. NDBH), will display the selected 35 Pokes meta. This is the community's preferred format for playing; other formats retain their lists of pokemon.

        - Certain extended metas modify global data like pokemon stats and move base power for convenience. This will carry over to other formats and in-battle tooltips. Select one of the main metas or turn the extension off to fix.
